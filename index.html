BUBBLE VS TEXT REPRESENTATION

This tests purpose is to measure the users reaction time when asked to click the smallest number or graphical bubble representation of that number. As a user your task is to complete the trials by clicking the smallest number or bubble as fast as you can. You must take as much time as you neet to get the correct answer. You will complete three trials for each value-representation totaling 18 trials for the entire experiment

Here are the test requirements:

1. Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox is required
2. A mouse or trackpad will be the method of input.
3. The experiment window is 400px by 400px so your display must be at least that size.
4. Experiment cannot be completed on a mobile device.


INSTRUCTIONS

For each trial select the smallest number or the smallest bubble. The bubble representation will be displayed first followed by the text representation. Create a text file and put your name on the top.
